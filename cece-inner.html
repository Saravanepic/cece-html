About Host

Embark on a serene journey to a beautiful travel destination and immerse yourself in the art of mandala-making. Join our workshop and immerse yourself in the therapeutic practice of creating intricate mandalas. Our expert facilitator Shalini will guide you through the process of drawing and coloring these beautiful patterns, allowing you to tap into a state of relaxation and calm.


Take a break from your daily routine and indulge in this therapeutic experience, surrounded by the beauty of your surroundings. This workshop is the perfect way to unwind and rejuvenate while creating a unique travel memory to cherish forever. As you focus on the intricate details of the mandalas, you will find yourself naturally drifting away from stress and anxiety, and into a state of peace and tranquility. No artistic experience is necessary – this workshop is suitable for anyone looking to take a break from their busy lives.

Overview

Cubbon Park is a major green lung of Bangalore. This self guided audio tour will take an interesting route that will cover all the major landmarks inside and on the periphery of this gentrified park. The park Spans several hundred acres, it encompasses thousands of trees and plants of hundreds of different species. You will also uncover several hidden gems along the route. Topics include the evolution of Bangalore; The British legacy; Trees of the park; Victorian-era buildings and the five famous statues inside the park. After winding our way through the shaded lanes of Cubbon Park, we will end the tour in front of Bangalore's most majestic building, the Vidhana Soudha.Read more about Cubbon Park Heritage Walk: A self-guided tour of Bangalore's unique history - https://www.viator.com/tours/Bangalore/Cubbon-Park-Heritage-Walk-An-audio-tour-of-Bangalores-unique-history/d5310-110804P322?mcid=56757
